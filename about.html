About Us

Furniture Frenzy was founded in Chapel Hill, North Carolina on December 11, 1789 with a passion for providing high-quality furniture at affordable prices. Our mission is to help customers create beautiful living spaces with our extensive range of furniture pieces.

We are proud to offer a wide selection of furniture styles, from classic to stylish, to suit the diverse needs and tastes of our customers. With a commitment to excellent customer service, we strive to make the furniture buying experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide high-quality and stylish furniture that meets the needs and desires of our customers. We strive to create a comfortable and inviting atmosphere in our store that inspires our customers to find the perfect pieces to enhance their home or office.

Company History

From humble beginnings as a small family-owned business, we have grown to become a trusted and respected name in the furniture industry.

Design Philosophy

Our design philosophy is centered around creating furniture that combines form and function, while emphasizing quality materials. We believe in timeless design that will enhance any space and bring joy to our customers.

Manufacturing Process

Our furniture is manufactured using modern technology while having attention to detail. From design to production, each piece is crafted with care by our skilled artisans to ensure the highest level of durability and aesthetic appeal.

Community Involvement

We believe in building strong relationships with our neighbors and strive to make a positive impact on the communities we serve. Whether through sponsoring local events or partnering with non-profit organizations, we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of those around us.

Team Members

Our team members are passionate about furniture and committed to providing exceptional customer service. With a wealth of industry knowledge and a friendly attitude, our team is always ready to assist our customers in finding the perfect pieces to fit their needs and style.

Awards and Recognitions

We are proud to have received multiple awards for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our furniture store has been recognized for excellence in design, innovation, and service, and we continue to strive for excellence in all aspects of our business.

Future Plans

In the future, our furniture store plans to expand our product lines to better meet the evolving needs of our customers. We will continue to invest in technology and design, while staying true to our core value of quality.
